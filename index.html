About The Company

Pikram's Vintage is a consulting company specializing in vintage fashion. We are here to help individuals and brands who want to showcase their unique identity through classic, elegant, and timeless clothing. By combining fashion trend research, cultural authenticity, and a personal approach, Pikram's Vintage has become a trusted partner for those who want to express themselves through retro to modern vintage fashion.

We do not merely provide clothing recommendations but also create experiences, from curating outfits and personal styling to developing fashion brand imagery centered around vintage themes.

Vision

To be the leading vintage style consulting company that inspires people to express their identity through timeless elegance and unique fashion.

Mision

✨Providing personal consulting and brand styling services with a touch of relevant and modern vintage style.

✨Educating the public about the historical value, beauty, and sustainability of vintage fashion.

✨Building a creative, inclusive, and empowered community of vintage fashion enthusiasts.

✨Providing quality clothing recommendations and curation tailored to clients' personalities, needs, and lifestyles.

✨Promoting vintage style as a sustainable fashion alternative that is environmentally friendly and aesthetically valuable.

Values

✨ Authenticity
Upholding the authenticity of vintage style and combining it with modern trends.

✨ Elegance
Bringing beauty and elegance to every styling detail.

✨ Personalization
Tailoring each recommendation to the unique character and needs of each client.

✨ Creativity
Presenting innovative, fresh, and inspiring style solutions.

✨ Sustainability
Supporting sustainable fashion through the introduction and use of vintage fashion.
